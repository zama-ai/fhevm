# Registration

Dapp developer and application registration process. 

## Flow

<!-- TODO: re-write this as a workflow diagram -->

- Dapp dev deploys a contract specific to call ZWS Oracle
- Dapp devs creates an account in the Console
- Registers given contract address + credit card (or other) on the platform
- We give a payload with a signature for said contract
- The dapp dev then calls the ZWS Oracle with the signature provided by ZWS
